About the Phone Parts Store

Founded in Christchurch, New Zealand in 2012 Breeze Imports started when, myself (Jamie Breeze) insisted that my friend bought her first decent Smartphone, a Samsung Galaxy S2. Within a few months, he had broken her third screen and the repair bill was now more than the price of a new phone. After some extensive research, I bought my first cell phone screen from overseas and took on the repair myself. As she continued breaking screens, I started keeping a stock to avoid life without her smartphone. Friends and family would break screens so I started selling to them, soon I built a small collection of phone screens which I took to the market.

Within two years and further research and understanding quality, we had over 500 products ranging from Samsung, iPhone, Nokia and Samsung. During this period I gave up my mechanical business to dedicate all my time to the large order quantities and forming relationships with repair stores all over New Zealand. In late 2015 I decided to bridge the gap into the Australian market.

I hope that in future we can build more relationships and give everyone the opportunity to repair their devices. We are also working on a range of Smart Technology that improve the lives of others whether it be simplifying peoples lives or improving the lives of the less fortunate. All this would not be possible if it wasn’t for the ongoing support from all our customers including all Technicians and Repair Stores.
